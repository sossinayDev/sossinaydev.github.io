Kumori

Kumori is an endless runner game, that plays in a downtown city. The graphics are made by Henryx (as always) and sossinay is coding the game. The game is progressing well, we expect it to be released soon, hopefully in May 2024

    How does it work?

    We as an indie gamedev team are heavily relying on feedback from players. To produce a good game, we are thankful for every feedback. If you want to playtest Kumori, please record your screen (including mouse cursor) and send it to this email: sossinayDev@gmail.com

    You can playtest the game, but if you want to help us you really should record the whole thing, and also don't ask other testers how the game works.
