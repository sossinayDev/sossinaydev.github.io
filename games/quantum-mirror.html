Quantum mirror

A game about making the perfect copy of yourself.

Mechanics / Features

In Quantum mirror, you have multiple clones which you can control. They get spawned one after another and are controlled individually.

You could also call it "teamworking with yourself"

There are several objects that you can use to solve the puzzle.

There are boxes, pressure plates, pistons and there's more coming.
