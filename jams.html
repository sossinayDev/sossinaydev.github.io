Gamejams!

Gamejams are a really cool opportunity for indie-developers like us to show our potential.

Just like contests, in gamejams you have to submit a game (often matching some theme or criteria) and then the jury chooses the winner(s).

Kinder-Game-Preis 2023

The Kinder-Game-Preis is a yearly event that offers indie devs up to the age of 16 to compete against eachother. The gamejam has no theme or prequisite so you just make a good game!

In 2023 Henry and I won the jam and got a price of 400 CHF. We agreed to split the money 50/50

The winner game was Spacecraft! a rouge-like action shooter in deep space, where you control a futuristic Spaceship, evade enemies and shoot back!
