Starfleet commander - Make 'em go boom!

In “Starfleet commander”, you take control of a 9-ship star fleet. Gather the scarce resources, fend off separatist ships. But watch out! A tiny little mistake can make your journey end early!

How to play

Important: This is how I do it. You can invent your own strategy. But I think this works well to get used to the controls.

  • Find a lava planet
  • Mine it by "dragging" your Lumatan ships over the planet
  • Leave one Lumatan there
  • Find a planet that gives you water and dirt. (See below for detailed planet list)
  • Mine it using your second Lumatan
  • 60 seconds in the first enemies will spawn
  • Make 'em go boom using your Q-27s. Keep an eye on your resources!
  • Find ice planet to regen your HP.
